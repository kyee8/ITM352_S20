Aloha! Welcome to Beautifully Designed. We are an Oahu based store that sells various designer bags. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of designer bags, with a focus on dependability, customer service and quality assurance. Founded in 2020 by Kristen Yee, Beautifully Designed has come a long way from its beginnings in a home office. When Kristen first started out, her passion for designing drove her to quit her day job, and gave her the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into to a booming online store. We now serve customers all over the world, and are thrilled to be a part of the desginer bags wing of the fashion industry. We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us. Sincerely, Kristen, Founder
